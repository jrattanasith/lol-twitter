Welcome to LoL Twitter

Welcome to the League Twitter Project. This is a project that I am working on where I basically create different Twitter profiles for various characters in the League of Legends Universe. Depending on how much fun I have, I may make this open source or something so that many other people can add on to it. Until then, profile content for each chatacter will feature something that I think Runeterra characters would have in contemporary times. I hope whoever reads this a large amount of pain and suffering while reading this.
